Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my generator services

The required generator capacity depends on your total power load. For small offices (10-20 kW), a 50-75 kVA generator is suitable. Medium businesses (30-50 kW) need 100-150 kVA, while large facilities (60-80 kW) require 175-250 kVA generators. I provide free site assessments to determine the exact capacity you need.

I recommend servicing generators every 250-500 running hours or every 6 months, whichever comes first. For generators used frequently, quarterly maintenance is ideal. My AMC packages include scheduled servicing, oil changes, filter replacements, and comprehensive inspections.

Yes! I offer 24/7 emergency repair services throughout Lahore and surrounding areas. My response time is typically 2-4 hours for emergency calls. I maintain a well-stocked inventory of common spare parts to ensure quick repairs.

AVR (Automatic Voltage Regulator) is a critical component that maintains stable voltage output from your generator. Proper AVR configuration prevents voltage fluctuations that can damage sensitive equipment. I specialize in AVR installation, calibration, and troubleshooting.

I service all major generator brands including Cummins, Caterpillar, Perkins, FG Wilson, Volvo Penta, Kohler, Generac, and more. I'm trained on multiple platforms and have extensive experience with generators ranging from 50 kVA to 250 kVA.
