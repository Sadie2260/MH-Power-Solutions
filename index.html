Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our generator services

The required generator capacity depends on your total power load. For small offices (10-20 kW), a 50-75 kVA generator is suitable. Medium businesses (30-50 kW) need 100-150 kVA, while large facilities (60-80 kW) require 175-250 kVA generators. We provide free site assessments to determine the exact capacity you need.

We recommend servicing generators every 250-500 running hours or every 6 months, whichever comes first. For generators used frequently, quarterly maintenance is ideal. Our AMC packages include scheduled servicing, oil changes, filter replacements, and comprehensive inspections to ensure optimal performance.

Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency repair services throughout Lahore and surrounding areas. Our response time is typically 2-4 hours for emergency calls. We maintain a well-stocked inventory of common spare parts to ensure quick repairs and minimal downtime for your business.

AVR (Automatic Voltage Regulator) is a critical component that maintains stable voltage output from your generator. Proper AVR configuration prevents voltage fluctuations that can damage sensitive equipment. We specialize in AVR installation, calibration, and troubleshooting to ensure your generator delivers consistent, clean power.

We service all major generator brands including Cummins, Caterpillar, Perkins, FG Wilson, Volvo Penta, Kohler, Generac, and more. Our technicians are trained on multiple platforms and have extensive experience with both diesel and natural gas generators ranging from 50 kVA to 250 kVA.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) provide scheduled preventive maintenance, priority emergency support, discounted parts and labor rates, detailed performance reports, and extended equipment life. AMC customers receive faster response times and reduced downtime, making it a cost-effective solution for businesses relying on continuous power.
